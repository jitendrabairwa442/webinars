Fitness Program is a good way to keep motivated

Some Methods Are

Vary the activity

If you are getting bored with walking, try swimming or an aerobics class.

Vary the place

Try a new route for walking or biking or even a different room for your exercises or stretching. By having several options, you can pick one that suits your mood or schedule.

Vary the time

Do your exercises at different times and for different amounts of time. If you become bored with your noon walk, try exercising in the early morning or after work or school. Instead of doing one 45-minute session, do three 15-minute sessions.
